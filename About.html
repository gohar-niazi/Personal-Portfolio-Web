About Me

I am Gohar Nawaz Niazi, a passionate and detail-oriented web developer based in Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan. I recently graduated with distinction from Gomal University, Dera Ismail Khan, earning a CGPA of 3.78.

In addition to my academic achievements, I proudly served as the President of the IT Society at Gomal University from September 2024 to May 2025. In this leadership role, I organized tech workshops, coding events, and peer-learning sessions that enriched the tech culture within the university.

My mission is to build impactful, clean, and responsive web applications. I’m always quick to learn new technologies, and I'm currently mastering the MERN stack along with TypeScript and Next.js to take my skills to a professional level.
